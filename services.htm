Our state-of-the-art technologies and methodologies allow us to produce quick comprehensive, quantified test results, generally 1-2 business days of specimen receipt to our lab, thus improving clinical outcomes for the healthcare provider and increasing patient safety.

Toxicology & Pharmacogenetics

We offer healthcare professionals the choice of toxicology testing via urine or oral fluid (saliva). PGX testing is done using a simple buccal cheek swab to extract DNA. It is KTM's goal to provide clinicians with state-of-the-art toxicology, high-throughput DNA analysis, concise reports and provider support. We enable providers to give their patients individualized treatment plans for personalized care and reduce overall healthcare costs.

Report Delivery Options

KTM delivers laboratory results through the following secure methods: HIPAA-secure fax line, online client login portal, encrypted electronic PDF results reports, and/or by Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Interfacing, where results are transferred continuously and directed to your patients’ medical records using standard HL7 protocol.

Technology & Research

We exclusively use UPLC-MS/MS for quantitative testing to provide some of the lowest validated cutoff levels in the industry for better detection of specific drugs and metabolites. Our in-house team of PhDs and clinical technologists are constantly developing and implementing assays based on client requests and pharmacotherapeutic trends.
