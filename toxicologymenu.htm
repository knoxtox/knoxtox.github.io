Knoxville Tox & Medical is constantly developing and implementing assays based on client requests and pharmacotherapeutic trends. Liquid Chromatography Tandem Mass-Spectrometry (UPLC-MS/MS) allows us to achieve higher chromatographic resolution, sensitivity, and greater throughput using sub-2-µm particle technology. Every specimen undergoes validity screenings to ensure normal ranges.
